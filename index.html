AMIRA
vision

Every story begins with an encounter. A craftsman, a talent, an entrepreneur, an ordinary person with something rare inside.

Words, photography, video, audio. The tool changes but the principle remains one: observe, tell, inspire.

Dedicated columns, collaborating with artists, creatives, intellectuals, people who love to share and inspire.

AMIRA vision is an editorial project founded on two principles: the power of sharing and the power of storytelling as forms of inspiration.

How it began
the project.

Fino a Dicembre 2020 non avevo mai letto un libro. Mi spiego: ne avevo letti tanti…da studiare, da memorizzare, da ripetere…
…ma non avevo mai letto un libro per piacere.

The first book I read for the pleasure of reading is called "The Usefulness of the Useless". That's how I discovered I enjoy reading. And the places I now love to explore are bookshops.

I have no favourite author, no favourite genre. I like to read what speaks to me in a given moment. As with music or cinema, at least for me, all I need is one thing:

a story that inspires me.

E un altro mondo dove continuamente scopro nuove storie è…
…il mondo.

Nel mondo, un po' per caso, un po' per destino, mi sta capitando di trovare delle belle storie. Queste storie si trovano in dei contenitori unici, che hanno infinite storie e storie nelle storie:
le persone.

And I thought, "If they help me, they can help others too. Don't let these stories die." OK.

I used to hate writing. I hated it. It bored me — but thanks to a high school teacher, I came to appreciate its value. And a book called "The Artist's Way" helped me find pleasure in daily writing (when I'm not feeling lazy).

Combining all of this with photography — also discovered partly by chance, partly by destiny — it doesn't explain life (because that's truly complicated to attempt) but it observes and tells it through people, talents, places, and beauty.

It is the meeting of many gazes, many visions of the world.

Ci saranno paragrafi brevi, e altri saranno più lunghi…con delle sospensioni…e momenti più veloci: sono i miei pensieri che si trasformano in parole, e non saranno mai perfetti.
(Com'è giusto che sia)

And then there will be the thoughts, emotions, and stories within stories of those who share a part of themselves.

Quando trovo la storia giusta, mi sento capito…
non so spiegarlo…
mi sento che sto andando nella direzione giusta (che poi qual è chissà…)
Spesso mi blocco e non trovo le risposte…
…ma se è vero che già abbiamo tutte le risposte dentro di noi, è altrettanto vero che a volte è necessaria una luce diversa e magari un altro sguardo per vederle.

AMIRA VISION is an editorial project built on two principles: the power of sharing and the power of storytelling as forms of inspiration.

Stories matter.
Discovering new stories enriches our own.
Your story can continue mine.

— Francesco
